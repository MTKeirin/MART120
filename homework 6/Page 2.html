Short Story

Okinawa!

I've never lived so close to the ocean. The breeze, sun, and sounds are delightful.

It's a little weird to walk around and say things like:

My ancestors could've walked here or I wonder if we watched the sunsets the same

I've never felt like that before. The difference between meeting and loving the generation directly above you

and thinking that 200 years ago someone who sorta looks like me was here is crazy.

It makes me feel more connected to history. Maybe this is what all of those family tree projects

were supposed to evoke.
